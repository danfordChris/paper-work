# Experience Model

## User

- Primary user: portfolio visitor evaluating Danford Chris through a single immersive landing page.
- User intent: scroll through a tactile, editorial showcase without navigation complexity.

## Experience Goals

- Present one continuous narrative from hero scene through signature footer.
- Make every scene feel tactile, paper-based, and editorial rather than dashboard-like.
- Keep interactions lightweight and local to a scene.
- Preserve desktop polish while remaining functional on mobile layouts.

## Non-Goals

- Do not behave like a document management product with real persistence.
- Do not require authentication, backend state, or multi-page routing.
- Do not introduce modal-heavy or form-heavy workflows into the landing page.
